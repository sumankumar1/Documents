Welcome to the OgreBill Complete User Guide — your go-to reference for everything in the app.

OgreBill is a professional invoicing app built for freelancers, solo entrepreneurs, and small businesses who want to look polished and stay organised without the complexity of enterprise software. Create beautiful branded PDF invoices in seconds, track expenses, monitor business health with real-time analytics, and share via email, WhatsApp, AirDrop, print, QR code, or local network — all from your pocket. No subscriptions required for core features, no internet needed, and your data never leaves your device.

🛡️ Important — Your data exists only on this device. OgreBill is 100% offline. Nothing is stored on any server or cloud. If your phone is lost, stolen, broken, or reset, your invoices and records are gone forever unless you have a backup. Please back up regularly to iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or another device. → Go to Backup & Restore

1. Getting Started — First-Time Setup
✨

The Setup Wizard (7 Steps)

When you open OgreBill for the first time, a step-by-step setup wizard guides you through configuring your business. Each step has a unique colour theme and a progress indicator at the top.

Step 1 — Welcome

A welcome screen introduces OgreBill's key features: professional invoicing, expense tracking, analytics, multi-sharing, and secure backups. Tap Get Started to begin.

Step 2 — Backup Destination

Choose where your backups will be saved. Pick a folder on your device — ideally inside iCloud Drive for automatic cloud sync. If a previous backup is found, you can restore it right here.

Step 3 — Location

Select your Country and Currency. OgreBill offers dedicated profiles for 10 countries — Hong Kong, United States, New Zealand, Australia, Canada, United Kingdom, Ireland, Netherlands, Sweden, and Switzerland — with tax labels, currency formatting, and field configurations set automatically. A Generic International profile is always available.

Step 4 — Business Information

Enter your business name, owner name, phone, email, and address. Optionally upload a business logo (from camera or photo library) — it will appear on all your PDF invoices.

Step 5 — Tax Configuration

Configure your tax rates. Country profiles pre-fill tax names (e.g. GST for NZ, Sales Tax for US). You can add multiple tax rates and set a default. You can also enter your Tax ID / GST number here.

Step 6 — Payment Methods

Add your bank details, PayPal, UPI, or other payment methods. You can attach QR code images to payment profiles. These appear on your invoices so clients know how to pay you. Optional — you can skip and configure later.

Step 7 — Network Sharing

Set up Wi-Fi network sharing for transferring invoice PDFs to nearby devices. Add trusted network names. Optional — you can skip and configure later.

Tip: You can always change any of these settings later from the Settings screen. The setup wizard simply gets you invoicing faster.
🎉

Setup Complete — Ready to Invoice!

After finishing setup you'll see a celebration screen. You can choose to Load Demo Data to explore the app with sample invoices, clients, and expenses, or jump straight in with a clean slate.

3. Invoices
📋

Invoice List Screen

The invoice list shows all your invoices with colour-coded status badges:

  • Draft — grey, still editable
  • Sent — blue, shared with client
  • Paid — green, payment received
  • Overdue — red, past due date
  • Cancelled — muted, voided

Features on this screen:

  • Search — type client name, invoice number, or amount to filter instantly.
  • Status filter chips — tap a status to show only those invoices.
  • Sort — sort by date, amount, due date, status, or invoice number.
  • Summary card — total invoiced, outstanding, and this month's stats.

Tap the + button (bottom right) to create a new invoice.

✏️

Creating / Editing an Invoice

The invoice form lets you build a complete professional invoice:

  1. Select a client — pick from your saved clients or add a new one on the spot.
  2. Add line items — for each item enter a description, quantity, unit price, and optional item-level discount (% or fixed). You can also pick from saved Services for instant fill.
  3. Invoice discount — optionally apply a discount to the full invoice (percentage or fixed amount).
  4. Due date — set when payment is expected.
  5. Notes — add any extra information or terms for the client.
  6. Review totals — subtotal, discounts, tax, and grand total are calculated automatically in real-time.
  7. Tap Save — the invoice is saved as a Draft.
Tip: Save frequently used items in the Services tab. When adding line items you can search and select them with one tap — no retyping needed.
🔍

Invoice Detail Screen

Tap any invoice to see its full detail. This screen shows:

  • Status badge with colour and icon
  • Client information — name, email, address
  • Invoice number & date
  • Due date — highlighted in red if overdue
  • Line items table — description, quantity, unit price, item discount, line total
  • Totals breakdown: Total before discounts, line item discounts, subtotal, invoice-level discount, tax, grand total
  • Notes
  • Audit log — a timeline of every action (created, edited, sent, paid, etc.)

Actions available:

  • Share — opens the Share screen with PDF preview (see Section 4)
  • Edit — modify the invoice (only when Draft)
  • Delete — remove the invoice (only Draft invoices — Sent/Paid/Cancelled cannot be deleted for audit integrity)

Status transitions:

  • Draft → Sent — mark as sent after sharing
  • Sent → Paid — record payment received
  • Draft / Sent → Cancelled — cancel the invoice
Tip: The audit log at the bottom is a complete history of every change — valuable for bookkeeping and dispute resolution.
4. Sharing & Exporting Invoices
🎨

The Share Screen

From any invoice detail, tap Share to open the Share screen. Here you see:

  • A live PDF preview of your invoice in the selected template theme.
  • A template picker — switch between 5 colour themes: Ocean Blue (default), Graphite, Emerald, Coral, Violet.
  • Your business name, logo, and contact details in the PDF header.
  • Your payment method details (bank, PayPal, UPI, QR code) in the PDF footer.

Below the preview are the sharing action buttons.

📨

All Sharing Methods

  1. Share via Email — Sends the PDF as an email attachment using your customisable email template. Recipient email is pre-filled from client data. (Premium feature)
  2. Share via WhatsApp — Sends the invoice PDF directly to your client's WhatsApp.
  3. Share via Device (Share Sheet) — Opens your device's native share sheet: AirDrop, Messages, Telegram, Signal, Files, clipboard, etc.
  4. Print — Print the invoice PDF directly to any AirPrint-compatible printer.
  5. QR Code — Generate a QR code that links to the invoice.
  6. Local Network Sharing — Share over Wi-Fi to nearby devices. The receiver scans a QR code to connect and download the PDF. Configure in Settings → Network Sharing.
Tip: A copy of the PDF is also saved automatically to your device's Documents folder with the naming format: invoice_YYYY-MM-DD_INV-XXX_ClientName.pdf

Client List Screen

  • Search — find clients by name, email, or company.
  • Sort — alphabetical, by most invoices, or by revenue.
  • Quick stats — each client card shows total invoices and outstanding amount.

Tap + to add a new client with their name, email, phone, and address.

Client Detail Screen

Tap any client to see their full profile and business metrics.

Contact Information: Name, email, phone, and address — with quick-action buttons to call or email.

Client Metrics Cards:

  • Total Revenue — total billed across all invoices
  • Outstanding Amount — total unpaid
  • Invoice Count — number of invoices for this client
  • Total Tax Collected — sum of tax from their invoices
  • Total Expenses — expenses linked to this client

Related Invoices: A full list of every invoice for this client. You can:

  • Filter by date range — presets: All, This Week, This Month, This Year, or Custom date range.
  • Sort — by date, amount, due date, status, or invoice number.
  • Tap any invoice to jump to its detail screen.

Actions:

  • Edit — update client details
  • Create Invoice — start a new invoice pre-filled with this client
  • Delete — remove the client (only if no invoices are linked)
Tip: The client detail screen is the best place to understand your relationship with a specific client — total revenue, what's owed, and their full invoice history, all on one page.
6. Services — Reusable Line Items
📦

Services List & Detail

Services are reusable line items that you can add to invoices with one tap. Each service stores:

  • Name / Description
  • Unit Price
  • Tax Rate (optional)
  • Default Quantity (optional)

Creating a Service:

  1. Go to the Services tab and tap +.
  2. Fill in the name, price, and optional tax rate.
  3. Tap Save.

Using Services in Invoices:

  1. When creating or editing an invoice, tap "Add from Services".
  2. Search or browse your saved services.
  3. Tap to select — the line item is filled automatically.
  4. Adjust quantity or price if needed for this particular invoice.

Service Detail Screen: Tap any service to view usage stats (how many invoices reference it), and edit or delete it.

Tip: If you sell the same products or services repeatedly, creating them here will dramatically speed up invoice creation.

10. Backup & Restore (Must Read!)
🚨

Why Backup Is Critical

Your invoices, clients, and financial records exist only on your device. OgreBill is offline-first — nothing is stored on a server.

Without a backup you could lose everything if your phone is lost, stolen, broken, factory-reset, or if the app is accidentally deleted.

Real risk: Even a routine iOS or Android update can sometimes cause data loss. A 30-second backup today can save months of work. Back up now — and back up often.
📁

Method 1 — Manual Backup (Recommended)

Create a backup file on demand and store it wherever you like.

  1. Open Settings → Data Safety.
  2. Tap "Set Backup Destination" and choose a folder. For cloud protection pick a folder inside iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or Dropbox.
  3. Tap "Create Local Backup Now".
  4. A timestamped .db file (e.g. backup_20260418T143022.db) is saved to your chosen folder.
  5. You will see a confirmation with the file name and size.
Tip: Store backups in a cloud-synced folder so they survive even if your device is lost. iCloud Drive is the easiest option on iPhone.
Where to find backups on your iPhone: All database backup files are also accessible in the Files app → On My iPhone → OgreBill folder. You can browse, copy, AirDrop, or move them from there at any time.
⏰

Method 2 — Automatic Backup

Let the app back up on a schedule so you never forget.

  1. Go to Settings → Data Safety.
  2. Make sure a backup destination folder is set.
  3. Toggle "Auto Backup" on.
  4. Choose a frequency: Daily (recommended), Weekly, or Monthly.
  5. Optionally enable "Incremental Backup" — saves only changes since the last backup.

Auto backups run silently. Check the last backup date on the Data Safety screen.

Where are auto backup files saved? They are regular .db files saved in the destination folder you chose — the same place as manual backups. On iPhone, open the Files app and navigate to your backup folder to see them. They are also always accessible in Files → On My iPhone → OgreBill. If your destination is inside iCloud Drive, the files sync automatically and are accessible on your Mac in Finder → iCloud Drive.
Note: Auto Backup is a premium feature available with OgreBill Professional.
📂

Method 3 — Import a Backup File

Restore from a backup file that lives anywhere on your device, or received via AirDrop, email, or messaging.

  1. Go to Settings → Data Safety.
  2. Tap "Import Backup from File".
  3. Browse to the .db file and select it.
  4. The app validates the file, then asks you to confirm.
  5. Your current data is replaced and the app restarts.
Tip: On iPhone, if someone AirDrops you a backup file it lands in Files → Downloads. Use "Import Backup from File" to pick it up.
🔄

Method 4 — Restore from Backup List

Browse all backups saved to your destination folder and restore any one.

  1. Go to Settings → Data Safety.
  2. Scroll to "Available Backups" — sorted newest first.
  3. Tap the backup you want to restore.
  4. Confirm. Your current data will be replaced.
  5. The app restarts with the restored data.
Important: Always create a fresh backup before restoring an older one — so you can go back if needed.
💻

Copying Backups to Your Mac or Computer

Every OgreBill backup is a standard .db file visible in the Files app on your iPhone.

Option A — iCloud Drive (easiest, automatic):

  1. Set your backup destination to a folder inside iCloud Drive.
  2. All backups sync to iCloud automatically.
  3. On your Mac: Finder → iCloud Drive — same folder, same files.

Option B — AirDrop:

  1. Open Files app → navigate to your backup folder.
  2. Long-press a .db file → Share → AirDrop → select your Mac.

Option C — USB cable (Finder):

  1. Connect iPhone to Mac with USB.
  2. Finder → select iPhone → Files tab.
  3. Expand OgreBill → drag backup files to your Mac.
Tip: Keep at least one backup on your Mac or external drive as a last line of defence.
📊

Export All Data as Excel

Export a human-readable Excel workbook with all your data:

  1. Go to Settings → Data Safety → Export All Data.
  2. An .xlsx workbook is generated with sheets for Invoices, Clients, Services, Expenses.
  3. Save or share the file via the share sheet.
Files → OgreBill folder: Exported Excel files and all database backups are also saved in the Files app → On My iPhone → OgreBill folder, so you can always find them even without opening the app.
Note: CSV / Excel export is a premium feature available with OgreBill Professional.
✅

Backup Best Practices

  1. Back up at least once a week — daily if you invoice frequently.
  2. Use a cloud-synced folder (iCloud Drive, Google Drive, OneDrive).
  3. Keep multiple backups — don't delete old ones too soon.
  4. Test a restore occasionally — verify your backups actually work.
  5. Export to Excel periodically — a spreadsheet is a readable safety net.
  6. Enable Auto Backup so you're protected even when you forget.
  7. Copy at least one backup to your Mac.
11. Frequently Asked Questions
☁️ Is my data stored online?

No. OgreBill is completely offline-first. All your data stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Data only leaves your device when you choose to export, share, or print.

⏳ What happens when the trial ends?

Basic invoicing features remain free forever. You can still create, view, and share invoices, manage clients and services, track expenses, and back up your data.

Premium features requiring OgreBill Professional: Analytics dashboard, Email sharing, CSV/Excel export, Auto Backup, Advanced tax configuration, Payment tracking.

🚫 How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS: Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → OgreBill → Cancel.

Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → OgreBill → Cancel.

Or tap "Manage Subscription" in OgreBill's Settings.

🔄 How do I restore a backup?

Option A — From backup list: Settings → Data Safety → scroll to "Available Backups" → tap one.

Option B — Import a file: Tap "Import Backup from File" → browse to any .db file.

Both validate, replace your data, and restart the app. Always create a fresh backup before restoring.

📥 How do I export all my data?

Settings → Data Safety → Export All Data. Creates an Excel workbook with all invoices, clients, services, and expenses. Individual invoices can also be exported as PDF from the Share screen.

🌐 Which countries does OgreBill support?

Dedicated country profiles for Hong Kong, United States, New Zealand, Australia, Canada, United Kingdom, Ireland, Netherlands, Sweden, and Switzerland — each with localised tax labels, currency formatting, and invoice field configurations. A Generic International profile is always available for any other country.

🔐 Why can't I delete a sent or paid invoice?

For audit integrity, Sent, Paid, and Cancelled invoices cannot be deleted. To void a sent invoice, change its status to Cancelled instead.

💎 What subscription plans are available?

OgreBill Professional: Monthly (pay as you go) and Yearly (best value). Both unlock all premium features. Managed via App Store or Google Play.

🔒 How do I lock the app?

Settings → App Lock — toggle on. Uses Face ID or fingerprint. Biometric data stays on your device and is never accessed by OgreBill.
